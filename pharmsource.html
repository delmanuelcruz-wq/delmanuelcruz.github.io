Context

The challenge

Independent pharmacy accounts don't buy on autopilot. Each order moves through quote building, margin checks, generic medication pricing verification, shipping-cost coordination, and finally fulfillment — across three internal departments and owner-level decision-makers on the customer side. At 603 accounts with high outbound volume, the difference between a clean month and a painful one is whether the operator catches pricing and shipping issues before the quote goes out.

What I did

Account coverage & revenue generation

  • Managed a portfolio of 603 independent pharmacy accounts across the US — executing 70+ outbound calls daily to owners and purchasing contacts to drive reorders, introduce new product lines, and maintain account relationships.
  • Generated $24,036.38 in sales revenue and $2,386.14 in gross profit across 39 billed orders in a 6-month period — converting daily outbound activity into documented, on-time purchase orders across 14 active accounts including Freedom Pharmacy, Carlisle Medical, and Westview Pharmacy.

Order lifecycle & cross-functional ops

  • Managed the full order lifecycle in Oracle NetSuite CRM — building customer quotes, processing confirmed sales orders, and tracking fulfillment from entry through shipment across every billed transaction, with zero billing disputes.
  • Coordinated with Purchasing, Accounting, and Sales before every order confirmation — verifying shipping costs and generic medication pricing so the quotes delivered to customers were accurate, margin-compliant, and ready for immediate processing.
Outcome

The result

A clean book of business with every transaction documented and every quote cross-checked before it left the building. Zero billing disputes across 39 billed orders isn't luck — it's the byproduct of doing the coordination work other people skip.

"Zero billing disputes" sounds small until you try to run 603 accounts across three departments without it.
What this shows

Skills demonstrated

  • CRM fluency in production — Oracle NetSuite quote building, sales order processing, and fulfillment tracking at real volume.
  • Cross-functional operations — Purchasing, Accounting, and Sales aligned before every confirmation, not after something breaks.
  • Margin and pricing discipline — verifying generic medication pricing and shipping costs so quotes are margin-compliant on day one.
  • High-volume outbound execution — 70+ dials per day converted into a documented pipeline, not just activity.
