Analyst Takeaway

What this dashboard is for

The real question leadership cared about wasn't "how many firms did we find" — it was "where should the outbound motion concentrate first." Filtering by category surfaces the two answers that matter: which verticals have the highest email-verification rate (lowest outreach friction), and which have deep contact populations (multi-threaded account motion possible).

That's the difference between a list and a go-to-market asset. The list is 833 rows. The asset is a prioritized sequence with a defensible reason per segment.
